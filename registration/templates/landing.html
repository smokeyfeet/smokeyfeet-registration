Smokey Feet 2016 Registration

Before the registration

Before signing up make sure you read the terms & conditions and level descriptions to see which level suits you best.

Sign up

You should receive an automatically generated message after your registration (note: this means we received your registration and does not mean you are accepted to the workshop). If this doesn't happen contact us!

Wait for confirmation and payment info

Your registration is initially put on hold until we can confirm that we can offer you a spot. Once a spot opens up you will receive an email. In this email you’ll find a link to a questionnaire and payment information. We hope to send out the first confirmations within a week after the registration opens.

Complete your registration

You have 14 days to complete your payment, fill in the questionnaire and send in your video audition (if applicable), or your reserved spot will be cancelled. You should receive a final email confirming your successful registration.

Important need-to-knows

Signing up with a partner

A lead and follow can sign up as a couple. Note that both of you need to sign up for the same level and you can not sign up with someone who’s already registered alone. Both of you need to mention your partner’s name during registration. Signing up with a partner is the best way to secure a spot for the workshop.

If you send your video audition, you will get an email with the information regarding which level you are accepted in.

It is possible to cancel or transfer your ticket. Check our Terms and Conditions for more info.
